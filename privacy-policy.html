Space-Duo is an arcade game developed by [Emre Yılmaz]. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

Space-Duo stores game data locally on your device only. This includes high scores, game preferences (sound settings), tutorial completion status, and daily challenge scores. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

Space-Duo does not currently use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No personal data is collected, shared, or sold to third parties.

Advertising

Space-Duo does not currently display advertisements. If advertising is added in a future update, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly, and any ad services used will comply with applicable privacy regulations.

Children's Privacy

Space-Duo does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The game does not require account creation, login, or any personal information to play.

Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device using standard web storage (localStorage). You can clear this data at any time by deleting the app or clearing app data in your device settings.

Permissions

Space-Duo does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other device features. The game requires no special permissions to function.
